Are You in Control of Your Gaming?

Responsible gaming means enjoying the experience while knowing when to pause. Please take a moment to thoughtfully reflect on the questions below:

  • Do you sometimes spend more time or money on gaming than you planned?
  • Do you continue playing to try and recover losses?
  • Have you ever borrowed money or sold something so you could continue gaming?
  • Has gaming affected your relationships, work, studies, or daily responsibilities?
  • Do you feel stressed, irritated, or anxious when you are not playing?
  • Do you use gaming as a way to avoid worries or personal problems?

If you answered “yes” to any of these questions, it may be the right time to review your gaming habits or reach out for guidance. Acknowledging a concern is a positive and courageous first step toward maintaining balance.
